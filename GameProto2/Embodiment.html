Part One: Embodiment

Story:

Doctor Polybius: Agent X is not like most of our agents. They are aware of their own multidimensionality, and they are able to move between worlds. They don’t have control over this power; they shift when dimensional tides or energies carry them away, but they’ve learned to ride these tides like a surfer on a wave. It’s remarkable, honestly. Through the years, Agent X has gained a reputation as an unorthodox investigator. In our dimension they may be following some seemingly mundane and unconnected task—eating from a bowl that isn’t there, straining to hear sounds no one else can, or recalling memories from a life they never lived. Meanwhile in another reality Agent X’s counterpart braves a hurricane to reach the ancient library that’s home to a spell-casting robot chef!
