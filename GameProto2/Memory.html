The Story

Crucius’s machines surrounded Agent X, whirring and humming with power and energy. Crucius laughed, enjoying their victory. “At long last, Agent X, we meet face to face,” they said, pacing around the perimeter of their hellish machine. “You’ve foiled me time and again, turned my glorious schemes to rubble. But now you’ve failed, my old friend.” Agent X looked around, seeking any way out. “A little premature, don’t you think?” Agent X replied. “We both know I’ve escaped worse.” Crucius smiled. “Not this time, I’m afraid. This is all for you; there’s no grander plan, no worlds to conquer, just a thorn to remove from my side. There is no escape”
